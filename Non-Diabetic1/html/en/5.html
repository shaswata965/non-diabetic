Keep Track

These changes will not happen overnight. The key to success is taking small steps that improve your health every day. These steps have to work for you – it’s your life and there is no one-size fits all solution!

Where to start? The first step to building healthier habits is becoming aware of what your current habits are.

  1. Track food: Simply add a picture of everything you eat – the good and the bad! Your health coach will keep track of how much you’re eating and give you dietary advice along the way.
  2. Track activity: Everyone has a daily step count goal to reach. Try to find simple ways to move more during the day and meet your daily goal. Having an active lifestyle is being active whenever possible, not only going to the gym for 30 minutes and sitting down for the rest of the day.
