Calories and Activity

You may not know this, but even breathing burns calories! Your body uses calories as energy to do every day work, like breathing, standing, and typing. But, to burn a significant amount of calories for weight loss, exercise is a key component..

Below you can find different ways to burn calories through activity. But remember, these numbers are not exact and depend on the intensity of the exercise and your weight.

✔ 1 mile of brisk walking (15 to 20 minutes) =100 calories
✔ 2 miles of slow walking (1 hour) = 150 calories
✔ Climbing stairs (15 minutes) = 150 calories
✔ Jumping rope (15 minutes) = 150 calories
✔ Water aerobics (30 minutes) = 150 calories
