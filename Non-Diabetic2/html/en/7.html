Balancing Act

While we don’t think it’s necessary to memorize calorie numbers, it may benefit you to know that every 1 pound of body fat stores about 3,500 calories.

Slow, steady weight loss is the best way to lose body fat. About 1 to 2 pounds per week is recommended. When you eat food, you consume calories. When you move around, you burn calories. Your weight is the balance between the calories you consume and the calories you burn. Losing weight requires that you burn more calories than you take in. Exercising and having fewer calories is the best way to tip the calorie balance in your favor and lose weight.

Calorie Balance

  • Weight is the same: Calories in = Calories out
  • Gaining weight: Calories in > Calories out
  • Losing weight: Calories in < Calories out

Droobi Tip

When you make changes that are part of your lifestyle, you are more likely to lose the weight and keep it off!
