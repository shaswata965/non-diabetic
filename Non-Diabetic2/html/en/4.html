Calories and Weight

In order to lose weight, you need to burn more calories than you take in from food and drinks.

A calorie is a measure of energy. Energy is needed to fuel body functions (heart beating, breathing, digestion and sleeping for example) and support physical activity like sitting, standing, running and doing household chores. Factors like gender, age, and height affect your daily calorie needs, as does your level of physical activity. .

Being aware of the amount of energy we take in the food we eat and the portion size is important in losing weight. We know that people who track what they eat are more likely to lose weight and keep it off!
